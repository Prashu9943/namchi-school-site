About Our School

Government New Secondary School, Namchi was established in 1995, has been a beacon of quality education in the region. We are committed to providing a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth.

Our school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and offers education from Class I to Class XII. With a team of dedicated educators and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to prepare our students for the challenges of the modern world.
